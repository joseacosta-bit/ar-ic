Event Logistics & Throughput

Processing 400-500 attendees through an active AR experience over two days requires strict timeline management and redundant hardware. We must calculate maximum theoretical capacity to ensure our target is feasible without creating massive bottlenecks.

Total Event Time 16 Hours (960 mins)
Session Duration 10 mins (incl. onboarding)
Simultaneous Users 6 Users per session
Max Theoretical Capacity 576 Attendees

Hardware Recommendation: Procure 10 total units. 6 active units for the live session, plus 4 backups continuously charging and being sanitized to guarantee zero downtime between 10-minute rotations.

Custom Development & Budget

Creating a synchronized, localized multiplayer AR environment requires a specialized development pipeline using tools like Unity, ARFoundation, and a spatial anchor service (e.g., Azure Spatial Anchors) to ensure all 6 users see the exact same holograms in the exact same physical space.
